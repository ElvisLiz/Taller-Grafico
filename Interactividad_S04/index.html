House Stark of Winterfell

Winter is Coming



Winterfell is the seat of the ruler of the North and the traditional home of House Stark. House Stark has held a castle at Winterfell for 8,000 years, though it has been considerably expanded over the centuries. Their sigil is a grey direwolf racing across a field of white. Their words are "Winter is Coming", one of only a few house mottoes to be a warning rather than a boast. Several of the POV characters of A Song of Ice and Fire are members of House Stark.

Eddard Stark

  • Eddard Stark was the head of House Stark and Lord Paramount of the North of the constituent regions of the Seven Kingdoms.

Lyanna Stark

  • Lyanna Stark was the daughter of Lord Rickard Stark and sister of Brandon, Eddard, and Benjen Stark.

Benjen Stark

  • Benjen Stark was the First Ranger of the Night's Watch. He embarks on a ranging north of the Wall, and did not return.

Robb stark

  • King Robb Stark was the eldest son of Lord Eddard Stark of Winterfell and his wife, Lady Catelyn.

Sansa stark

  • Sansa Stark is the eldest daughter and second child of Lady Catelyn and Lord Eddard Stark.

Arya stark

  • Arya Stark is the youngest daughter and third child of Lady Catelyn and Lord Eddard Stark.

Bran stark

  • Bran is the fourth child and second son of Lady Catelyn and Lord Ned Stark.

Rickon stark

  • Rickon was the fifth child and youngest son of Lady Catelyn and Lord Eddard Stark.

Jon Snow

  • Jon is the son of Lyanna Stark and Rhaegar Targaryen, the late Prince of Dragonstone.
